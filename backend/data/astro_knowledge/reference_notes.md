# Astrology Reference Notes

## Sun
The Sun represents your core identity, ego, and life purpose. It is the conscious mind and the creative force that drives you forward, shining a light on where you are meant to take center stage.

---
## Moon
The Moon governs your emotional inner world, instincts, and subconscious patterns. It reveals how you nurture yourself and others, pointing to your deepest needs for safety and comfort.

---
## Mercury
Mercury is the planet of communication, intellect, and exchange. It shapes how you process information, articulate your thoughts, and connect with the immediate environment around you.

---
## Venus
Venus is the planet of love, beauty, values, and attraction. It colors your approach to relationships and romance, and highlights what you find pleasurable and worthwhile.

---
## Mars
Mars is the planet of action, desire, and assertion. It represents your drive, physical energy, and how you pursue what you want, as well as how you handle conflict.

---
## Jupiter
Jupiter represents expansion, luck, higher learning, and philosophy. It points to where you experience growth, optimism, and an innate sense of abundance in life.

---
## Saturn
Saturn is the planet of structure, discipline, limitation, and responsibility. It shows where you must put in hard work and build a solid foundation over time to achieve lasting mastery.

---
## Uranus
Uranus brings sudden changes, innovation, and rebellion. It represents your need for freedom, originality, and the areas in life where you are meant to break the mold.

---
## Neptune
Neptune governs dreams, intuition, spirituality, and illusions. It blurs boundaries, inviting you into a world of profound imagination and empathy, while warning against escapism.

---
## Pluto
Pluto is the planet of transformation, power, death, and rebirth. It strips away what is no longer needed, forcing deep psychological regeneration and uncovering hidden truths.

---
## Aries
Aries is the pioneer of the zodiac, carrying a fiery, cardinal energy that is bold, courageous, and direct. It thrives on taking initiative and leading the charge into the unknown.

---
## Taurus
Taurus is an earth sign characterized by stability, patience, and a deep appreciation for the senses. It is a fixed energy that builds lasting security and enjoys the simple, beautiful things in life.

---
## Gemini
Gemini is an air sign known for its curious, adaptable, and communicative nature. It thrives on variety, intellectual stimulation, and exchanging ideas with a vibrant community.

---
## Cancer
Cancer is a watery, cardinal sign deeply connected to home, family, and emotional roots. It is incredibly nurturing, protective, and relies heavily on intuition and empathy.

---
## Leo
Leo shines with a fixed, fiery warmth, radiating confidence, creativity, and a dramatic flair. It is a sign that leads with the heart and naturally commands attention and respect.

---
## Virgo
Virgo is a mutable earth sign focused on service, detail, and self-improvement. It brings a practical, analytical approach to life, always seeking to refine and perfect its environment.

---
## Libra
Libra is a cardinal air sign that seeks harmony, balance, and partnership. It values fairness and aesthetics, striving to create peace and beauty in all its relationships.

---
## Scorpio
Scorpio is a fixed water sign characterized by intensity, passion, and emotional depth. It is unafraid of life's mysteries and possesses a profound capacity for transformation and resilience.

---
## Sagittarius
Sagittarius is a mutable fire sign driven by a quest for truth, adventure, and higher knowledge. It is fiercely independent, optimistic, and always looking toward the horizon.

---
## Capricorn
Capricorn is a cardinal earth sign known for its ambition, discipline, and pragmatic approach to success. It focuses on long-term goals and is dedicated to building a lasting legacy.

---
## Aquarius
Aquarius is a fixed air sign that is innovative, objective, and deeply concerned with humanity. It values intellectual freedom and envisions progressive changes for the collective.

---
## Pisces
Pisces is a mutable water sign, boundlessly empathetic, artistic, and deeply spiritual. It flows with the currents of emotion, effortlessly connecting with the unseen realms of the universe.

---
## 1st House
The 1st House, or the Ascendant, represents your self-image, physical appearance, and the immediate impression you make on others. It is the lens through which you first interact with the world.

---
## 2nd House
The 2nd House rules your personal resources, finances, values, and self-worth. It points to what you possess and how you cultivate material and emotional security.

---
## 3rd House
The 3rd House governs communication, short trips, siblings, and your immediate environment. It reflects your learning style and how you exchange ideas in your daily life.

---
## 4th House
The 4th House is the foundation of the chart, representing home, family, ancestry, and your private inner life. It is your emotional base and sense of belonging.

---
## 5th House
The 5th House is the realm of creativity, romance, play, and children. It highlights how you express your joy, take risks, and share your unique talents with the world.

---
## 6th House
The 6th House focuses on daily routines, health, service, and habits. It represents how you organize your life, care for your body, and the everyday work you do.

---
## 7th House
The 7th House rules one-on-one partnerships, marriage, and open contracts. It shows what you seek in a significant other and how you navigate the balance of giving and receiving.

---
## 8th House
The 8th House governs shared resources, intimacy, transformation, and the mysteries of life. It points to deep psychological healing and the merging of energies with others.

---
## 9th House
The 9th House is the house of higher learning, philosophy, long-distance travel, and belief systems. It shows how you expand your horizons and search for greater meaning.

---
## 10th House
The 10th House, or the Midheaven, represents your career, public reputation, and major life achievements. It is your legacy and how you are recognized by society at large.

---
## 11th House
The 11th House rules friendships, networks, community, and your hopes for the future. It is about the groups you align with and the collective goals you strive to achieve.

---
## 12th House
The 12th House is the realm of the subconscious, hidden matters, spirituality, and endings. It represents a need for retreat, surrender, and connection to the universal whole.

---
## Conjunction
A conjunction occurs when planets are very close together in the same sign, blending their energies into a powerful, unified force. It marks a concentrated focus and the beginning of a new cycle.

---
## Trine
A trine is a harmonious 120-degree angle that allows energies to flow effortlessly and naturally. It brings ease, talent, and supportive conditions, though it can sometimes lead to complacency.

---
## Square
A square is a tense 90-degree angle indicating friction, obstacles, and internal or external conflict. This aspect demands action and growth, forcing you to overcome challenges to achieve strength.

---
## Sextile
A sextile is a supportive 60-degree angle offering opportunities and friendly interactions between planets. It requires a bit of effort to unlock its potential, but it brings positive communication and ideas.

---
## Opposition
An opposition is a 180-degree angle that creates a tug-of-war between two opposing forces. It highlights the need for balance, compromise, and integration of polarized energies, often playing out in relationships.

---
## Mercury Retrograde
Mercury retrograde is a period when the planet of communication appears to move backward, often bringing delays, miscommunications, or technological hiccups. It is a powerful time for reflection, reviewing, and rethinking rather than pushing forward.

---
## Venus Retrograde
Venus retrograde is a period for reassessing relationships, values, and finances. Past loves or financial issues may resurface, asking you to gain deeper clarity on what you truly value and desire.

---
## Rising Sign
Also known as the Ascendant, the Rising Sign is the zodiac sign that was on the eastern horizon at the exact moment of your birth. It dictates your outward demeanor, the "mask" you wear, and your initial approach to life.

---
## Midheaven
The Midheaven (MC) is the highest point in your chart, representing your highest aspirations, public image, and career path. It shows what you are striving toward and how you wish to be remembered.

---
## North Node
The North Node represents your karmic destiny and the life lessons you are meant to learn in this lifetime. It points toward areas of growth that may feel unfamiliar or challenging but are ultimately fulfilling.

---
## South Node
The South Node represents your past life karma, innate talents, and comfort zones. While these are skills you naturally possess, the evolutionary goal is to move away from over-relying on them and journey toward the North Node.
